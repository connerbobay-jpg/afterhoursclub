The Concept

When the day is done,
this is your time

After Hours Club is a curated series of evening events designed for young adults and busy moms in the Carmel area. Whether you just clocked out or just got the kids to bed, we created a space for you to breathe, move, and connect with your community. Think wellness meets social hour — with really good coffee.

Community

A space to meet your people, make real connections, and have the kind of night you actually look forward to.
